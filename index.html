Featured Systems / 重点项目

Projects are explained with actual evidence and context.

Flagship Artifact

CroqTile

CroqTile is a GPU and accelerator programming language centered on TileFlow and DMA-oriented abstractions. It targets high productivity without sacrificing performance or low-level control.

  • Achieves 5x productivity and around 40% of equivalent CUDA code
  • Matches or approaches vendor-level performance with near-zero abstraction cost
  • Includes 353 compile-time checks and 1,319 runtime assertions
  • Supports AI-driven tuning, including 671 to 1127 TFLOPS in 68 iterations on a published case

Recent Research Line

Structured Sparsity and MoE Acceleration

Current technical pre-research on structured sparsity computation and MoE acceleration at Enflame. This line is already tied to visible open-source artifacts and recent paper outputs.
